In the JSON, you can define hotkeys for your timers.

Use the format: 
"hotkey":"<Keycode>"
OR (if you want modifies  like Shift, Ctrl, Alt, Meta (Windows key))
"hotkey":"<Modifiers>-<Keycode>"

Examples:
"hotkey":"3" (3 key)
"hotkey":"NUMPAD3" (number pad's 3)
"hotkey":"S-NUMPAD3" (Shift + Number pad's 3)
"hotkey":"SC-NUMPAD3" (Shift + Ctrl + Number pad's 3)
